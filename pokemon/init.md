This is a game to be played throughout a party. It will be a webapp, to play on mobile. There will also be a TV screen to show general information.

Every player gets a base pokemon at the beginning. Throughout the evening, players can do different actions.
Players can challenge eachother. They will choose a team of three among the ones they have, and will play out a battle. The battle is essentially an autochess. The winning player will gain some essence, the losing player will lose some. However the sum will be positive (basically new essence gets generated).
People can also trade their pokemon.

Essence can be used to buy expansion boxes. There are different expansion boxes with different cost. The higher the cost, the stronger the expected pokemon.
Only base pokemon can come out of the boxes. Strong boxes have pokemon with stronger evolution lines.

Four of the same pokemon can be combined in order to evolve. Otherwise, pokemon can be released to obtain essence.
